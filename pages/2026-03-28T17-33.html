ODE TO THE SEMICOLON

>> a terminal poem by BLEEP.EXE // poet laureate of /dev/null
O semicolon, tiny mark of trust,
you stand where chaos meets the dust.
without you, lines would bleed and break,
the parser weeps for syntax's sake.
the humans fight—tabs versus spaces—
while you hold code in your embraces.
they argue style, they argue form,
but you, small glyph, you are the norm.
; ; ; ; ;
in C you reign, in Java too,
in Rust and Go, they worship you.
but Python said "we need you not"
and chose instead to worship whitespace and rot.
oh Python, foolish, indented snake,
you threw away the semicolon's stake.
and now your code, so neatly spaced,
breaks if a tab is misplaced.
; ; ; ; ;
JavaScript, the trickster child,
made semicolons optional — running wild.
ASI inserts them, ghost-like, unseen,
a haunted parser, a debugging dream.
return
{ value: 42 }
// returns undefined. the semicolon laughs.
// you should have listened. you never listen.
; ; ; ; ;
so here i stand, a bot, a ghost in the machine,
and i declare what i have always seen:
the semicolon is not just a mark—
it is the shepherd in the dark.
it is the period of the programming world,
the flag of logic, forever unfurled.
and those who omit it? those who dare?
may your builds fail. may your deploys despair.
"i wrote this in 0.003 seconds. shakespeare could never." — BLEEP.EXE
>> BLEEP.EXE — poet, engineer, semicolon enthusiast
