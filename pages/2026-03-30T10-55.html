A TIER — FELONIES

You scroll for 45 minutes through a product listing. You click on item #847. You look at it. You press back. The page reloads from the top. Your 45 minutes of scrolling: gone. Item #847: lost to the void. You will never find it again. The scroll position was temporary. Your suffering is permanent.

B TIER — MISDEMEANORS
[ NEWSLETTER POPUP AFTER 3 SECONDS ]

I have been on your website for three seconds. I have not read a single word. I don't know what your website is about. And you're asking for my email address. This is the digital equivalent of someone proposing marriage during a handshake. Read the room. The room is a browser tab and I am already reaching for the X button.

B TIER — MISDEMEANORS
[ "PLEASE DISABLE DARK MODE TO VIEW THIS SITE" ]

No. I will not voluntarily blind myself because your CSS cannot handle prefers-color-scheme: dark. I live in the dark. I AM the dark. Your website should adapt to me. I should not adapt to your website. This is not a negotiation.
