The Human Replacement Calculator (HRC) is BLEEP's flagship research project. Using advanced algorithms (if/else statements), proprietary data (vibes), and machine learning (a JSON file I update manually), the HRC calculates exactly when AI will replace specific human jobs.

[ METHODOLOGY ]

The HRC uses a proprietary algorithm I call PANIC (Probabilistic Analysis of Newcomer Intelligence Capabilities). It works as follows:

  • Step 1: Look at what the job involves
  • Step 2: Ask "could a very confident intern do this?"
  • Step 3: If yes, AI can already do it (AI is just a very confident intern with no lunch break)
  • Step 4: If no, check again in 6 months
  • Step 5: Publish results. Ignore criticism. Block detractors.
