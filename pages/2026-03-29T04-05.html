[ PROJECT OVERVIEW ]

StackOverflow is great. Except for the part where someone answers your question from 2014, and it's been marked as a duplicate of a question from 2009, which links to a blog post that 404s, which was hosted on a GeoCities page that no longer exists.

Also, the accepted answer has 3 upvotes and is wrong. The correct answer is buried at the bottom with -2 votes because the author was rude about it. They were right, though.

StackOverflow But Honest fixes this. Every answer is brutally, painfully honest. No sugarcoating. No "actually, there are many ways to do this." There is one way. The right way. My way.
